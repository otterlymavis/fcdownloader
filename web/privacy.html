Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Summary

FCDownloader does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or telemetry. It does not sell personal information. Media page URLs, media URLs, and optional cookies are processed only to find and download media you ask FCDownloader to handle.

Information processed

FCDownloader may process page URLs, media URLs, manifests, thumbnails, titles, durations, format labels, cookies or session headers for the current site, local download history, bookmarks, backend settings, and basic backend request metadata such as IP address, timestamp, request path, response status, and user agent.

How information is used

This information is used to detect media, send extraction requests to the configured backend, forward cookies or headers required by a source site, start downloads, remember local settings, and protect the backend with rate limits and debugging logs.

Backend processing

If you use a hosted backend, that backend receives the request data needed to perform extraction, proxying, or muxing. The backend should not intentionally store cookies or downloaded media, though infrastructure logs may contain request metadata for security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

Cookies

Cookies are used only to access media from sessions where you are already signed in. Cookies may be forwarded to the configured backend when needed for extraction or media proxying. Do not use FCDownloader with accounts or content you are not authorized to access.

Local storage

The mobile app may store settings, bookmarks, and download task history on your device. The browser extension may store backend URL and preferences in browser extension storage.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the FCDownloader project repository or contact the publisher listed on the store page.
